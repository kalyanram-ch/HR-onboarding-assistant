HR Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What are the company working hours?
A: Standard working hours are 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM Monday to Friday.

Q: How many annual leave days do employees receive?
A: Employees receive 20 days of paid annual leave each year.

Q: How do I request leave?
A: Leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal and approved by your manager.

Q: What benefits does the company provide?
A: Benefits include health insurance, retirement plans, training programs, and wellness initiatives.

Q: Who should I contact for HR support?
A: Employees can contact the HR department through the HR helpdesk or email support.
